Supplier Registration

At Sri Lanka Cricket, we are committed to fostering strong partnerships with suppliers who share our dedication to quality, integrity, and innovation. By becoming a registered supplier, you'll join a trusted network that plays a vital role in supporting the growth and success of cricket in Sri Lanka. If you provide goods or services that meet our standards, we invite you to register with us and be part of our journey toward excellence!

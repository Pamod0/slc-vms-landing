Frequently asked questions

Any business or individual offering relevant goods or services that meet SLC’s standards for quality, integrity, and professionalism is eligible to register. The following entities and individuals are eligible to register as suppliers with Sri Lanka Cricket:

  • Limited liability companies
  • Partnerships
  • Proprietorships
  • Foreign incorporated companies
  • Individuals

Suppliers must align with the categories listed on the SLC website. For the full list of categories and subcategories, visit www.srilankacricket.lk/suppliers-registration

Yes, existing suppliers must re-register through the new online system for the year 2025. This ensures that all supplier records are updated and maintained accurately in the new system. Re-registration is mandatory for all suppliers, including those previously registered.

Yes, registration fees apply as follows:

  • New Local Suppliers: Rs. 5,200.91/- per main category + Rs. 1,000/- per subcategory + taxes.
  • Foreign Suppliers: USD 17/- per main category + USD 3/- per subcategory + taxes.
  • Existing Suppliers (Renewals): Rs. 2,600.45/- per main category + Rs. 500/- per subcategory + taxes.

All required documents must be uploaded through the online portal at www.vms.mycricket.com.lk. If additional documents are needed, suppliers will be notified through the system.

Yes, suppliers can register under multiple main and subcategories. Fees will apply for each category selected.

Yes, all required documents must be uploaded together to complete your registration. We recommend gathering softcopies of all necessary documents beforehand to ensure a smooth and efficient registration process. Incomplete applications will not be processed.

Yes, annual renewal is mandatory to maintain an active and updated supplier status.

Yes, foreign suppliers are welcome to register through the same online portal at vms.mycricket.com.lk. They must comply with the same registration process and provide the necessary documentation.

If your application is rejected, the reason for rejection will be displayed on the system. This may occur if the submitted documents or information are incorrect or do not match the requirements. You will have the option to resubmit the correct documents and information. Ensure that all details are accurate and complete before resubmitting your application. Applications must be resubmitted before the deadline to be considered for registration.

If you are blacklisted from the system, it means you have not complied with SLC’s quality standards or have violated terms outlined by SLC. Reasons for blacklisting may include:

  • Failure to meet the agreed quality and service standards.
  • Non-compliance with contractual obligations.
  • Unresolved payment issues or disputes.
  • Any other concerns deemed significant by SLC.

If you have been blacklisted, you should contact info@srilankacricket.lk to inquire about the reason and the necessary steps to resolve the issue.

  • Email: info@srilankacricket.lk
  • Call: +94 112 681 601-4
